Thank you for participating to the Green Challenge 2020!

Congratulations on completing the 2020 Green Challenge! As this year’s challenge brought all new content, all new formatting, and an all new working context, we’d like to recognize the cross-departmental effort that it took. A special thanks to Transport Canada and Environment and Climate Change Canada for their close collaboration with the Centre for Greening Government team in drafting the interactive and educational content.

About the Green Challenge

This year, we broke records. You were one of the 2,523 public servants from 59 federal organizations that participated – over twice the amount from last year! Parks Canada came in at number one with 433 participants. Way to go!

As a whole, over 747,505 points were collected. Congratulations to Parks Canada for winning the top spot for the most points recorded and to the Office of the Auditor General for achieving the highest participation rate: 33.9%!
